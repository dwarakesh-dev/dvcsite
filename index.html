How does the NFC technology work?
The DVC card contains a near-field communication chip. When tapped against a smartphone, it triggers a link to your digital profile automatically. No app is required.
What if someone doesn't have an NFC-enabled phone?
Every DVC card also features a custom QR code on the back. They can simply open their camera app and scan the code to view your profile.
Can I change my details after the card is printed?
Yes! That is the core benefit. You can log into your dashboard and change your links, phone number, or job title anytime, and the card updates instantly.
